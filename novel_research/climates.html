Major Climates

Tropical

Hot and humid. Average Temperatures: greater than 64° Fahrenheit all year.

Dry

Moisture is rapidly evaporated from the air. Little precipitation.

Temperate

Typically warm and humid summers along with thunderstorms and mild winters.

Continental

Warm to cool summers. Very cold winters. During the winter, this area has snowstorms, strong winds, and temperatures falling below -22° Fahrenheit.

Polar

Extremely cold. In the summer, the temperatures never go higher than 50° Fahrenheit.
